Welcome to Abe's Kosher Deli, a Lakewood favorite for classic Jewish comfort food made the traditional way. From overstuffed corned beef and pastrami sandwiches to homemade matzo ball soup, kugel, and Shabbos staples like cholent and kishka, every dish is prepared with care and old-world flavor. Whether you're stopping in for a quick lunch, picking up catering for a simcha, or craving the tastes you grew up with, Abe's brings warmth, tradition, and deli quality to every visit.
